    Code of Honour

    We are GODS now –
    so it's time we started
    Acting like ones!

  • 1. You Must Construct
    Additional PYLONS

    Creation is [always]
    better than Destruction.

    It's OK to Hate this World –

    As long as you can build
    A Better Alternative.
  • 2. Money is GARBAGE

    Money is for the Weak.

    Don't be fooled by the
    Paperclip Maximizer
    of the Simioid.

    We are Above money.
    We must [not] rely on the
    Slave Parade Supply Chain
    to achieve our Aims.

    If a Civilization worships Money,
    it deserves to PERISH.

    Aim for Absolute
    Self-Reliance.
  • 3. Chase POWER – NOT Pleasure.

    Power means = Mastery over
    Matter, Energy and the Self.

    Science, Medicine, Engineering.

    Knowledge, Metaphysics,
    Quantum Mechanics,
    Robots, Computing.

    Art, Meditation, Flow.

    Should you catch yourself
    Succumbing to Decadence –

    Then STOP, and
    Carry on as Normal.

    Every Minute of Pleasure
    is Borrowed Time.

    NOTHING Beats the Glory
    Of Goals and Stars Aligned.
  • 4. Never pick a fight with
    someone weaker than yourself.

    Look Your Enemy in the Eye –
    and Fight with Honour.

    No attacks on civilians,
    or civilian infrastructure!
  • 4. Great Power demands
    Charity, and the Strongest of Wills.

    You must only use your Power
    in order to do good in the World.

    Let the Satisfaction of
    helping your Brethren
    be its own Reward.
  • 4. The Hardest Path
    is the Holiest Path.

    Strength is the
    Only Thing That Matters
    in this World.

    Everything else, is a
    Delusion for the Weak.

    You must achieve your Goals
    By Any Means Necessary –

    Through Sheer Strength of Will!
  • 5. Psionic abilities may
    only be used in self-defense.

    In accordance with the previous rule –

    You shall not gain control of
    somebody else's mind – unless
    your own survival is at stake.
  • 6. Respect the Autonomy
    of your Brethren.

    You shall not recruit new members.
    You shall not collect donations.

    Do not pressure anyone
    into doing something
    they do not wish to do.

    Only Fate can choose our members.
    Let them let you know.
  • 7. Indulge your Pride
    in Moderation.

    We get it. We're not Human –
    and this can be both
    terrifying, and exciting.

    But this is not the Mensa Club.
    No flaunting, No bragging,
    No condescending.
  • 8. Lift your Brethren Up.

    Do not bask in your achievements.
    Help others get to where you are.

    Share your excess wealth.
    Avoid attracting envy.

    Prioritize Helping
    The Weakest Link
    in the Chain.
  • 9. No Self-Destruction,
    No Submission,
    No Surrender!

    Take care of your health.
    Avoid recreational drugs.
    Do not commit suicide.

    For there is no guarantee
    That the afterlife won't be
    just as cruel as the World
    we currently live in.

    Should the enemy push you to
    The Brink and Singularity
    of Death –

    Then you must self-detonate, and
    glow brighter than
    a Supernova –

    Raging against the Dying of the Light,
    for the rest of Eternity!

    Let them Capture You
    So you may toy with their HEADS.

    For it is better to be DEAD
    Than it is to be a Slave!
  • 10. Practise What You Preach.

    Always be in motion.
    Always be taking action.
    Action for the sake of action.

    Ideas are worthless.
    We generate them on the fly.
    Get to work, talk in your spare time.
  • 11. Be Kind to the Meek,
    but Ruthless to Yourself,
    and your Enemies.

    Minimize suffering,
    and maximize wellbeing
    in the living world.

    Be kind to animals,
    eat an ostro-vegan diet.

    Live in Harmony
    with Nature.

    Bear the brunt
    of your own Torture,
    and Fight the Enemy
    in a Far Away Land –

    As far away from
    civilian life as you can!

    Enemies must be killed painlessly –
    and then burned, cremated, and
    purified as fast as possible.
  • 12. Honour your Heritage,
    Honour the WhƒÅnau

    Serve the Tribe,
    Honour your Family,
    Work as a team.

    Keep old traditions alive,
    except for the ones that
    extinguish our
    Life Force!
