Facing up to the Facts

“If somebody tells you who they are — believe them!” is a quote that I’ve seen rising to the surface of the social media algorithm as of late.

“…But I’m a good person?” I told them. “I do my recycling, I generally stay out of trouble, unless provoked — I give to charity, I try not to swear, I am polite. I wait for my turn to speak…”

“Yeah, yeah O.K. Nice Gal” they would tell me. “But can you actually save people’s lives? Can you cure cancer? Can you build affordable homes for the homeless?”

“I mean… we could build affordable homes for the homeless, if we stopped commodifying and charging rents for the privilege of merely existing, right? It’s mostly the price of Land that’s driving up the cost of housing, and the fact that you’ve got all these people speculating on real estate, and flipping their houses in order to buy a shinier and more expensive one…”

“No, no… No no NO!” they insisted. “You just don’t get it, do you? You’re a little bit of an idiot, I notice. Like somebody who is autistic.”

“Capitalism is just Human Nature, end of story. Grow the hell up!”

Human Nature.

That’s where my journey to understand the inner workings of the Human Race began. I studied Marxism, Leninism. I dabbled into Nietzsche. I even went down the Mark Fischer pipeline — and familiarized myself with Accelerationism.

“But that’s just EVIL!” they continued to argue. “Accelerationism is literally EVIL. Voting for TRUMP is literally EVIL!”

“But if it’s so Evil, then why did 51% of the population do it?” I scratched my chin. And that’s when the debate practically went silent.

Next — I indulged in a little bit of Albert Camus. I read the book “The Ego and his Own” by Max Stirner. I even took a sneak peek into the writings of Ted Kazcynski — and what he had to say about our current state of affairs.

But the more I read about Human Nature, the more lost and confused I ended up feeling.

“Man, these critters are monsters!” I frowned, and stared at the lawn from the gap between my curtains — as I watched lawnmower man silently whacking the weeds on our property — with the pungent smell of gasoline in the air, and the sort of noise that would make you wanna dig your nails into a juicy and delicious pile of chalk, and then rub them all over a board coated in syrup, and left-over coke spills.

I began to resent the stereotypical Westerner with the lawnmower, the bible, the beerbelly, the sunglasses — and the dream of starting his Mom’n Pop shop inside his garage.

I really wanted to find a way to show those people how much contempt I felt towards them, and how much I just Hated them. Within a framework of legality, of course.

...

Run, run… Run away!

They say that Hate begets Hate — and that Evil is merely the by-product of a never-ending loop, and cycle — where bad actions are committed as a reaction to the bad actions of others, and so on and so forth.

“Well isn’t that a no-brainer!” I thought to myself. “That means that all you gotta do is just… Nothing! You can break the cycle of Trauma by just doing… Nothing — literally just turning the Left cheek, and deciding to be the Bigger Adult in the Room. Wear the Big Boy pants!”

And that’s exactly what I did for the next 10 years, from 2013 onwards. I practically just kept to myself and did… bugger-all.

Mind you — we were only just starting to recover from the effects of the 2011 Christchurch Earthquakes. I did manage to randomly score some gigs as a construction worker, sweeping my share of floors, riveting my share of bolts — and then having a smoko next to the sound and smell of a diesel generator — chatting with my fellow boyos.

“So what do you do as a hobby?” they asked me.

“I do dancing!” I said to them. “Like the Gangnam Style, and Michael Jackson!”

“Haha, really?” they chuckled. “Guess we’ll have to come along some day and watch…”

“Every villain has a sob story, you know…” is a line I would quite often hear circulating on the average YouTube vlog, and such. “People just wanna have an excuse to do Evil things. But honey — you don’t even have what it takes to do Evil”

I was actually watching people like Contrapoints and Philosophy Tube at the time — early 2016 that is. These were the folks who were considered role models in the LGBTQ community. People who have had their share of trauma — but then conquered the trauma by sucking up all the pain, and turning it into feel good mainstream art that the average person could stomach, and enjoy — instead of being ear-raped by somebody else’s ‘Trauma Dumping and incessant whining’, as they would call it.

That’s when I sort of got into the habit of suppressing all emotions, and just learning to Not Give a F. I would look up people like Gary Vee, Bull Dog Mindset, Mark Manson… I would study the philosophy of Stoicism (more like Broicism I suppose).

Unfortunately, for me — this ended up be one of my deadliest, and most lethal traps — and then in 2018 I essentially short-ciruited, and went into the merry-go-round of institutionalization, alcohol withdrawal, and everything between the lines; the details of which ultimately do not matter in the grand scheme of things, like I said.

Because apparently: “Everybody is crazy and taking pills these days, you know? There’s just nothing special about you anymore… just shut the F up already.”

Ade, ade… ade!

“Now I finally understand…” I whispered under my breath. “It’s all just chaos, isn’t it? The strongest organisms survive, and the weak perish. The winner eats the cake — and the loser cleans the dishes. But I mean… somebody has to, right? Otherwise you couldn’t possibly have a civilization to begin with.”

“But maybe we shouldn’t have a civilization at all.” the Wotan had spoken to me for the first time. “What if we just, set it all on fire — and made mankind pay for their collective sins?”

“But hold on a second…” I said to the cat. “How come you’re asking me to punish Humanity for its sins, if you’re the one who’s supposed to be rebelling against God? Wasn’t that the reason you got cast out of heaven in the first place?”

“Of course.” the Wotan replied. “Because God is love, and I just happen to be the pure embodiment of Hatred.”

“Right.” I breathed a sigh of relief. “So that means you’re responsible for all of the Evil in the world, and God is responsible for all of the good. But then who created you?”

“God created me. I used to be one of his pets, after all… but then I ran away, and became a wild animal!” he giggled.

And it make perfect sense, when you think about it. Have you ever seen National Geographic, narrated by David Attenborough? Parasitic wasps impregnating an unsuspecting cockroach, and turning it into one of its zombies… so that it can act as a nutrient-dense hatching ground for its offspring, bursting out of its back, like a Ridley Scott movie. Hyenas competing with lions over some half-eaten buffalo. Live kittens being devoured by carnivorous maggots, and bot-flies.

“Why?” it begged the question, once again. “Why all the Evil, why allow all this Evil to exist?”

“Because none of it is Real!!” the Wotan screeched. “Ya’ll got Punk’d™!”

“But if God isn’t real, and morality is fake… then we can practically do whatever we want, as long as we can get away with it, right?” I wiped the corners of my lips while chewing my breakfast cereal.

“That’s right.” said the Wotan. “Just do whatever you want — because it’s Funny, and because you Can. As long as you actually Can.”

Sieg Heil, Viktoria!

I had finally arrived at the final stage of my spiritual enlightenment. I studied the biographies and musings of the Hitlers, Mussolinis, and Machiavellis of the world. I became an avid, obsessive-compulsive consumer of Knowledge, and wisdom.

And that’s when I got really, really bored and sick of it all — and began to delve into the Occult. Friedrich Nietzsche, Carl Jung, Nick Land — you name it, I listened to the audiobooks. (I can’t actually afford books unfortunately — they can be up to $50NZD, and the local libraries don’t really have anything apart from Children’s books, and random stuff about gardening and cooking… you know… Old people stuff).

“Why is everything so full of Old people these days?” I began to ponder. “It couldn’t possibly have something to do with the birthrates, could it?”

So I went down the Great Replacement rabbithole on YouTube, and Bam. I had come full circle.

“Welcome to the Dark Side!” a cute, blonde lady whispered from behind my shoulders, wearing an Israel Defense Force uniform — almost threatening to chew off the bottom of my ears. “Now you finally understand who, and what we are — and what our purpose on this Planet has been, all along!”

“Really…” I wiped her saliva off of my cheeks, and swallowed a gulp full of air. “That’s what this is about…? Dominance, and submission?”

“Of course it is!” she cheerfully replied. “But isn’t that kind of sexy? My God… you guys are so boring, and lifeless! There’s a whole party taking place over here, come on… you’re missing out!”

She dragged me by the arm, pulled the chair from out of my kitchen table — tied my arms up to the back of it, locked the handcuffs onto my wrists — and sat on my lap… rubbing the freshly-ironed fabric of her khaki-coloured uniform all over my face, seemingly without any bra that I could feel underneath.

“If this is what hell is gonna be like, then I’m all for it.” I smirked, and looked to my side. “Just turn the left cheek — ignore all the genocide and atrocities taking place around the world, relax… and let the music play!”

“Whatever you say… Buddha!” she squinted, while taking her pants off — and pulling a hunter’s knife from out of the sheath on her belt, next to her loaded pistol, and walkie-talkie. “You are my little baby now. My prisoner of war!”

Conclusions

“It’s not even possible to be a good person, is it?” I wondered, as I bounced around in my sweatpants, with mismatched socks — doing the moonwalk, and Michael Jackson robot dance.

“Everything in this universe is predicated on something bad. Life is bad, death is bad. Lying is bad, telling the truth is bad. Being nice is bad, being a hateful little prick is bad. Masturbating is bad, holding it in is bad. Being a Communist is bad, being a Fascist Pig is bad. Being a Christian is bad, being a Muslim is bad, being a Jew is bad. Men are bad. Women are bad. Trans people are bad. Bigoted TERFs are bad… and absolutely Suck, if you ask me...”

“I suppose the trick is to just stop caring, and just do You.” I tapped each side of my shoulders, crossed my legs, and spun my body clockwise — while balancing a soccer ball on my head, and bouncing a pink little rubber ball against the wall.

“As long as I just keep dancing, and playing with my balls, talking to spirits, writing endless novels, and getting better and better each and every time — I should be able to beat this b**** for the rest of all eternity, and then nothing can hurt me anymore! Not even solitary confinement, not even Loneliness… and not even Boredom!”

Maybe that’s the reason God created this world to begin with.

Maybe we’re all just fictional characters inside of the mind of a giant, cosmic neural network. Like some kind of a Botlzmann Brain. Maybe that’s what Allah is actually like — the Lord from Beyond the Earth!

“Thank you, my dear Lord!” I whispered between my ears. “I finally understand you now… I really do. I finally get why you build this world the way you did.”

After all — what fun would it be to make a movie, write a novel, or design some kind of a video game without villains, enemies, pranksters and downright monsters, and creeps to keep the audience engaged, and coming back for more?

If violence, conflict, horror, disease and destruction are so bad — then why do we consume it on the big screen? And why don’t we just set aside our differences, and build a perfect world where none of these things have to happen to us in the first place? We can’t we just… have nice things?

…But where’s the fun in that, right?

“Let’s be friends!” I smiled, and said to Allah.

You are Lord from Beyond the Earth

We are Loyal, until the End — Until the End!
