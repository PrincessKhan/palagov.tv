Morning Nausea

I woke up this morning with a loud ringing in my ear, and a heavy sense of confusion about my surroundings.

Prior to that — I had found myself stuck inside of the allegorical Minotaur maze, running away from a male IDF (Israel Defense Force) soldier who had been scouring the whole building with a hunter’s knife, calling out my name; and out for blood.

It was like some kind of a swimming pool changing room, but shaped like a maze. There were benches and locker rooms everywhere, so plenty of spaces to hide — but also plenty of spaces to be ambushed, and taken by surprise.

I had a backpack with me, as well as a small pouch full of tools — so I pulled out my hack saw, and a pair of scissors — and went on the prowl, looking to see if I could take out the Minotaur.

I stepped inside of the Men’s bathroom, and then turned on the faucet to wash my hands, being careful not to make too much noise.

All of the sudden — the door slammed wide open, and the Minotaur walked right in — pointing his knife right under my neck.

“Alright, fine!” I sighed, and yelled at him. “Let’s have sex!”

The Minotaur took two steps backwards, lowered his knife — and then slowly pulled his pants down, followed by his underwear — and lo and behold, there it was: a rather tiny, small… and disappointing Sight.

“So?” he stood there looking at me, all embarrassed and awkward.

“I think I’ll pass, thanks.” I whispered, and then made my way out of the toilet as fast as I could.

It would be fair to say that I’ve already conquered most of the demons that have haunted me in this world, spare for the final two that have gotten the best of me, chiefly — the demon of Loneliness — and the demon of Boredom.

I can handle just about anything that life throws at me. Ocean waves, spiders, heights, getting T-boned by a car on my moped, COVID-19, earthquakes, bullying at school, bullying at the workplace, etc.

Yet none of that has ever translated into any sort of success in my life, and instead — I now live in a world where it’s practically impossible to really make any meaningful connection with anyone whatsoever. No matter how much grass you touch… no matter how much grass you smoke… no matter how much you self-improve, or how many languages you learn.

The fact of the matter is that, nobody cares — and nobody really owes you anything in this world.

The only thing you can truly rely on is Yourself, but even that will eventually just sink you further and further into bottomless rabbit holes of despair — which in turn renders you into an even shallower, and more embittered shadow of your former self.

I used to think that watching so-called ‘Manosphere’ content would make me into a better person. I thought that I could maybe steal some of those ideas, and apply them to my own life, and try to be a less ‘triggered’ sort of individual. You know — try to be less offended, and less of a ‘special snowflake’. Which is one of the things that people on the conservative Right were complaining about, non-stop.

“Fine!” I yelled at the clouds, all the way back in 2017 — when Donald Trump first became president of the United States of America.

“I’ll stop being a special snowflake, and buy myself a MAGA hat. And then go around posting frogs, and doing Nazi salutes on YouTube, and all that stuff. And then see if people will finally be friends with me!”

But it turns out — it was all just another trick by the Wotan to try to mess with my mind, and carry on torturing me.

Because now it’s actually the conservatives who are walking around being special snowflakes, and complaining about antisemitism, and online hate. You can’t win, can you?

“Alright then.” I said to myself this morning. “I’ll stop being an Edgelady, and go back to being myself. No more caring about what people think, and no more following random ideas and ideologies that do not align with the very core of my being!”

But now, of course, it’s a little too late — the damage has been done.

All those people who supposedly called themselves ‘Warriors 4 Jesus’ were nothing more than sockpuppets for the Wotan… and now there’s no way in Hell that I’ll ever be able to make a connection with anyone whatsoever.

I got played, and I got played rather cleverly.

And I only have myself to blame.

Preparations

They say that one man’s Utopia, is another man’s Dystopia.

“A boring and sad day for you, would be an Earthly paradise for a family trying to survive in Gaza today”

And indeed — they were right. I am an ungrateful piece of garbage, and I don’t deserve anything good in this world. All that self-improvement and toughening up has only made me more toxic, and less desirable to others — not more — and now it feels as if my heart is slowly beginning to malfunction, with palpitations lasting all the way up to the 15-second mark.

It’s like you just can’t win, and I don’t know what to do anymore. I am wholly convinced that I already find myself trapped inside Hell, and that I’m about to find out what’s at the very bottom of it.

“You should have just put down the phone, and quit social media then!” But the things is — I already tried that back in 2013-2017 — back when I was visiting bars and nightclubs on a daily basis, and doing small talk with strangers in public places to try to improve my social skills.

It really didn’t make any difference, and those were actually some of the worst years of my life. Not that it really matters all that much.

All things are Nothing to me — and I am just about getting ready to go and burn in the deepest circle of Hell — so that I can at least have some kind of structure, and predictability to my existence.

I just want something in my life to make sense, you know? I want something to at least be explainable using my own words, and my own interpretation of what on Earth is happening to me, and what the Hell is going on.

So this is what I’m going to do, every single Wednesday night from now on.

I am going to donate exactly 34USD to a random charity, all around the world. For 63 weeks straight. And then I’ll go back into my robotics lab — and carry on doing my Works, for the rest of the week.

Abandon all Hope, ye who come here!

I am ready to cave in, and let the Wotan take my soul — but I won’t allow him to take anybody else’s.

I shall spend the rest of eternity being incinerated, frozen and then incarcerated in a super-positioned, Analog Inferno — where I will endure the agony of everlasting Boredom, and Loneliness.

And I’ll take my punishment Like a Man. Not like some wimpy, overly-apologetic YouTuber with a Ukulele.

Apologizing is for the Weak.

A Goddess does not apologize for her actions — and considering the damage I’ve inflicted unto others over the years, and the ease with which I pulled it all off — it seems that I possess far too much Power and Privilege to ever allow myself to be thought of as some kind of a victim.

I am not a victim.

I am the Victimizer.

I regret nothing. I stand by my actions, I stand by every single thing that’s ever come out of my mouth — and I couldn’t have asked for a better fate.

I did it because I was bored, and because I had nothing better to do.

I did it for the Lols— and to keep the plot moving, I suppose.

I did it it all for Myself. Because I love myself.

I did it because it was genuinely Funny.

Now let me go to Hell, and suffer in Peace.
